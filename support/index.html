FAQ

Is Barcode Lab an official carrier application?
No. Barcode Lab is an independent testing utility.

Can generated labels be used operationally?
No. Generated outputs are fictional test data for validation only.

What barcode families are in scope?
The app currently focuses on business test scenarios including RM JGB6/JGB8 and related QR/DataMatrix workflows.
